Messengers of Hope

Who would come to some of the most neglected refugee camps on Earth, and volunteer to teach, and bring smiles to children's faces? Shot on location in Nablus, Palestine, we tell the story of the volunteers who are at the center of Project Hope. During the filming, the City Hall in Nablus was attacked again, jets flew overhead on their way to bomb Lebanon, and the carnage in Gaza continued. In the face of these obstacles, our local and international volunteers continued their work, bringing Nablus and to the camps their message of hope.
