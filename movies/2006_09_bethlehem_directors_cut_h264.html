Bethlehem 2006

Bethlehem is a really beautiful city, albeit tragically under-touristed. Take a tour through Manger Square, see kids at a summer camp, and see a Palestinian fashion show!

This is a re-edited version of a video originally uploaded in July 2006. The original was edited in Nablus on a PC with a bad sound card; I've fixed the sound and re-done the editing on Final Cut.
